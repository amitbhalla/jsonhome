Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free strategy call?

The free 30-minute strategy call focuses on understanding your marketing challenges, business goals, and current situation. We'll discuss how my services might help address your needs and what next steps might look like. This is also an opportunity for you to ask any questions about my approach and experience.

How do you typically structure client engagements?

Client engagements can take several forms depending on your needs, including:

  • Project-based consulting for specific initiatives
  • Ongoing advisory retainers for continuous strategy support
  • Workshops and training sessions for your team
  • Fractional CMO services for companies needing executive marketing leadership

After our initial discussion, I'll recommend the most appropriate engagement model based on your situation.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes, I work with clients globally. Most consulting work can be done remotely via video conferencing and collaboration tools. For clients requiring on-site work, we can discuss arrangements that make sense for both parties.

How quickly can we start working together?

My availability varies depending on current client commitments. Typically, I can begin new engagements within 2-4 weeks of agreement. For urgent needs, I may be able to accommodate faster start dates or provide interim advisory support while we formalize a longer-term engagement.
